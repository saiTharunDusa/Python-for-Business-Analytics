
Data dictionary for credit.csv file

(Currency is NT = New Tiawanese dollar)

LIMIT_BAL: Amount of the given credit (NT dollar): it includes both the individual consumer credit and his/her family (supplementary) credit.

SEX: Gender (1 = male; 2 = female).

EDUCATION: Education (1 = graduate school; 2 = university; 3 = high school; 4 = others).

MARRIAGE: Marital status (1 = married; 2 = single; 3 = others).

AGE: Age (year).

PAY_1 to PAY_6:
History of past payment. We tracked the past monthly payment records (from April to September, 2005) as follows: 
PAY_1 = the repayment status in September, 2005; 
PAY_2 = the repayment status in August, 2005; 
. . .;PAY_6 = the repayment status in April, 2005. The measurement scale for the repayment status is: -1 = paid full; 1 = payment delay for one month; 2 = payment delay for two months; . . .; 8 = payment delay for eight months; 9 = payment delay for nine months and above.

BILL_AMT1 to BILL_AMT6: Amount of bill statement (NT dollar). 
BILL_AMT1 = amount of bill statement in September, 2005; 
BILL_AMT2 = amount of bill statement in August, 2005; . . .; 
BILL_AMT6 = amount of bill statement in April, 2005.

PAY_AMT1 to PAY_AMT6: Amount of previous payment (NT dollar). 
PAY_AMT1 = amount paid in September, 2005;
PAY_AMT2 = amount paid in August, 2005; 
. . .;PAY_AMT6 = amount paid in April, 2005.
